About OBEY FOOD'S

Bringing authentic Nigerian flavors to your doorstep with love, tradition, and excellence.

Our Story

OBEY FOOD'S was born from a passion for authentic Nigerian cuisine and a desire to share our rich culinary heritage with food lovers everywhere. Founded in 2020, we started as a small family kitchen with big dreams.

Our founder, Chef Obey, grew up learning traditional recipes from her grandmother in Lagos. She understood that food is more than sustenance – it's a connection to culture, family, and home. That's why every dish we prepare carries the authentic taste of Nigeria.

Today, OBEY FOOD'S has grown into a beloved local brand, but we've never forgotten our roots. We continue to use traditional cooking methods, source the finest ingredients, and prepare every meal with the same love and care as if we were cooking for our own family.
